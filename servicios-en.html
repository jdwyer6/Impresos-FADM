Products and Services

Food Industry

In the Food Sector, we design and create packaging for food products using a wide range of special food-grade materials and export-type coatings, ensuring safe packaging for all markets. Our packaging solutions meet the highest quality standards in the food industry, aimed at protecting and extending the shelf life of products from transportation to the shelf.

Automotive

We provide assembled packaging solutions for the transportation and handling of specific materials and parts. Our packages are custom-designed and tailored to meet set requirements: from thermoformed products to boxes with integrated cells that minimize and optimize the available storage space for products.

Electronics

We produce integrated packaging with the best specialized antistatic protection for sensitive and high-value electronic products. This way, we provide the optimal packaging to safeguard all types of products and circuits from moisture and mitigate shocks and discharges that could be harmful.

Perfume and Cosmetics

We manufacture exclusive and personalized packaging according to the highest standards of the global beauty market trends. We offer an extensive variety of packaging options, featuring the most attractive and functional designs in the industry that reflect the unique value and vision of each brand.

Medical and Pharmaceutical

We provide specific packaging for medical and pharmaceutical supplies in accordance with the strict guidelines of the industry. Our sanitary-grade designs enable efficient and orderly stacked storage, effectively maintaining the product's warranty and shelf life from packaging to the end customer.

Toys

We create packaging with the best designs and a wide variety of consumer-friendly materials for the sector, without sacrificing the protection and presentation of the product during its transportation and large-scale storage.

Stationary

We create packaging with designs specific to customer needs. Our packaging stands out for having the highest quality in manufacturing and materials within the industry, with dynamic solutions that protect the integrity of each stationery and writing product.

Metal

We produce packaging of the highest quality during the manufacturing process, utilizing the most advanced techniques in the market, scalable to any size.
